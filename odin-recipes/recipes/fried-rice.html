Fried Rice

The Perfect Weekly Prep

Not only is this dish delicious, but it's low in calories and high in protein! This dish is perfect for a weekly meal prep, simply put in the microwave for 2 min, let cool, and you should be ready to eat a tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • Egg Beaters
  • Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  • Green Onions
  • Peas and Carrots
  • Ready Rice
  • Chicken Tenderloins

Steps

  1. Cut 672g of chicken into bite size pieces
  2. Place on a baking tray (add non stick spray) and put in oven for 400 degrees for 15 min.
  3. Put 4 packets of fried or jasmine rice into the microwave for 3 min 30 seconds.
  4. Take chicken out of the oven and spread the rice on top.
  5. Add 170g of peas and carrots on top of rice.
  6. Pour 60g of low sodium soy sauce on top of the food.
  7. Put back in the oven at 400 degrees for another 6 minutes.
  8. Scrammble 184g of egg beaters
  9. Take the food out the oven and add the now scrammbled eggs on top.
  10. Sprinkle 15g of green onions onto the food
  11. Evenly seperate all of the food together and into 5 meal prep containers. (You can weigh out each container if wanted)

Macros

Seperated into 5 containers, each will contain 473 calories with 42 grams of protein.
